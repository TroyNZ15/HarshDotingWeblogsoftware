Overview

The purpose of the project is to design a low-poly character on blender.

This character will be used in a video game and will have the ability to move around.

This character is a medium height, elvish archer armoured up for battle and will be a favourite amoungst players.











End Users

After the completion and finalisation of the Poly Character. The design would of been incorporated into a video game.

The End Users, the players of the game themselves would be able to use this character as an avatar. These people are consumers of gaming products and are active gamers who usually spend a large amount of their free time towards video game.

They like want from the poly character a good design and something that looks good.

They need to have characters inorder for them to play the game, another need would be the need to diversify the amount of characters to distingush players.

A want would be the ablility to customise such characters.
